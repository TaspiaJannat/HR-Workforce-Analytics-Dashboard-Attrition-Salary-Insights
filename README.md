## üîç Key Insights & Analysis
The dashboard provides a 360-degree view of the workforce, focusing on demographics, attrition, and payroll trends:

### 1. Workforce Demographics
* **Total Headcount:** 1,470 Employees (Active: 1,233).
* **Gender Distribution:** 501 Females vs. 732 Males.
* **Education Breakdown:** The highest headcount (473) is found in the top education tier.
* **Employment Type:** Comparative analysis between **Full-time** and **Contractor** roles.

### 2. Attrition & Retention (Termination Analysis)
* **Attrition Rate:** The overall termination rate is **19%**.
* **Quarterly Trends:** Termination peaks in **Q3 (72 employees)**, while new hiring is highest in **Q4 (323 employees)**.
* **Monthly Peak:** **July** saw the highest number of exits (34), suggesting a seasonal turnover trend.

### 3. Financials & Payroll
* **Total Salary Paid:** A cumulative analysis of monthly payrolls.
* **Peak Payout:** **October** recorded the highest salary expenditure ($864,867).
* **Salary Spread:** Detailed visualization of salary distribution based on years of experience for both contractors and full-time staff.

### 4. Employee Sentiment
* **Job & Environment Satisfaction:** The dashboard highlights that the majority of employees fall into the high-satisfaction category (391 & 386 respectively), indicating a healthy organizational culture.
